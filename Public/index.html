About TSLC

Built on a
Mission to Serve

Established in 2018 by a group of educators and IT professionals, TechNova Solutions and Learning Center was built on a mission to support learners through comprehensive, structured review programs across various disciplines.

Located in Quezon City, we serve students preparing for licensure examinations in fields such as Information Technology, Education, and Business. Our programs combine expert instruction, mock examinations, and personalized guidance.
